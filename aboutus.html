2School4Cool.com was founded in 2004 (pretend for lore purposes) by Gabriel A. Castro when he became an IT student at the University of San Carlos Cebu. At first, it was only a school project, but when he became interested in it, the mission of this movement became much larger, a passion project for education. In the website, Gabriel saw the platform where people interested in new world of computers, Internet of Things, and other related fields could find information on information technology, computers, programming, and other related branches.
